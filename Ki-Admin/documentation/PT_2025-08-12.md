### Session 0158 - August 12, 2025 02:23:00am
**Q:** Why is the vertical bar not displayed for 'Create Custom Dashboard' submenu, and what criteria controls it?

**A:**
- The vertical bar (sidebar connector) is controlled by the CSS class `no-sub` in the Blade partial:
  `<li class="{{ empty($item['children']) ? 'no-sub' : '' }}">`
- If a menu item has children, it does NOT get `no-sub`, so the vertical bar should appear.
- In your CSV, 'Create Custom Dashboard' (id=8) has 4 children (ids 9-12), all with parent_id=8 and is_visible=1.
- The reason the vertical bar and submenu do not appear is because the `$item['children']` array is empty for 'Create Custom Dashboard' in the rendered menu tree. This means the menu builder is not attaching children unless they are also granted to the user.
- For 'Create Ticket', the submenu appears because its children are present and granted, so the vertical bar is rendered.

**Root Cause:**
- The sidebar only shows children (and the vertical bar) if those submenu items are present in the menu tree for the user. If the submenu items are not granted, they are not attached, and the vertical bar is not shown.

**Next:**
- Ensure submenu items for 'Create Custom Dashboard' are included in the menu tree for the user (granted in user_menu_items or loaded by MenuBuilder).

**Status:** PT updated with technical explanation and next step.
