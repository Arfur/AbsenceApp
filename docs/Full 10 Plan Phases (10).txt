PHASE 10 — FINAL INTEGRATION & QA (BLAZOR VERSION)
Goal:
  - Validate every V2 system (design system, layout, templates, tables, theming, services, modules, framework extraction).
  - Ensure the Absence app remains fully functional with zero regressions.
  - Ensure all V2 components work independently and safely.
  - Ensure all changes from Phases 2–9 are additive, isolated, and reversible.
  - Prepare a migration plan for future adoption of V2 components.

Constraints:
  - Do NOT modify existing V1 pages, components, CSS, or services.
  - Do NOT replace existing routing or layouts.
  - Do NOT migrate any pages yet.
  - Only fix issues inside V2 components.
  - All validation must be done in isolation using V2 demo/test pages.
